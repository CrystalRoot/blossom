Blossom Game Helper

This site is meant to assist players of Merriam-Webster's Blossom daily word game select the best words possible to maximize their scores without automating the entire process.

Rules

  1. Words must include at least four letters.
  2. Words must use the center letter.
  3. Words may use the same letter more than once.
  4. No proper nouns, hyphenated words, or foul language.

Scoring

  1. Four-letter words = 2 points.
  2. Five-letter words = 4 points.
  3. Six-letter words = 6 points.
  4. Seven-letter words = 12 points.
  5. Each additional letter = +3 points.
  6. The petal outlined in yellow (which changes with every word played) contains a bonus letter. Bonus letter = +5 points per use.
  7. Pangrams (words which contain all 7 letters) = +7 points.

Input today's letters, including the required middle letter and the bonus letter.
